# brand-corforge-products

**Brand:** CorForge  
**Domain:** https://corforge.com (apex) and https://www.corforge.com  
**Category:** Composite rigging + marine hardware (sailboats and watercraft)

## Purpose
This repository hosts the public-facing product definitions, documentation, and marketing-ready assets for **CorForge**.

CorForge focuses on composite and hybrid hardware such as:
- sailboat rigging components (blocks, cleats, travelers, rudders)
- cross-watercraft hardware (oar locks, straps, benches, mounting systems)

## Open Designs / Closed Process
Designs are intended to be open source. Manufacturing processes and production parameters are not published and remain proprietary.

## Ecosystem intent
CorForge parts may be used on JDP-owned brands and on other watercraft where the designs offer unique features or performance.

## Commerce & Memberships
Early sales may occur via eBay as market tests.  
Ecommerce and memberships will be handled by **WaterWolf** (private platform).

## What’s in this repo (initial)
- `/site/` – static website content (landing pages and product pages)
- `/catalog/` – product placeholders (to be populated as SKUs and accessories are finalized)
- `/LICENSE` and `/LICENSES/` – licensing scaffold (per your standard)

## Status
Pre-launch. Content will expand as initial hardware SKUs become available.
