Overview

CorForge develops composite rigging and marine hardware for sailboats and other watercraft, including blocks, cleats, travelers, rudders, and structural accessories.

Early sales may occur via eBay as market tests. Ecommerce and memberships will be handled by WaterWolf (private platform).
